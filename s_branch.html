Year of Establishment: 1993
Grades: Nursery to 10th
Total Strength: 1524 Students
Principal: Mr. Patil B.B. (B.Sc., B.Ed.)

The school is situated on the Ghoti Highway in Sinnar, Nashik, providing easy access for students and parents. It is equipped with modern classrooms, a library, science labs, and computer facilities, ensuring that students have the resources they need to excel in their studies.

The school is also committed to the safety and well-being of its students, with CCTV coverage throughout the campus and a dedicated staff that ensures a secure learning environment. The administration maintains an open line of communication with parents, ensuring transparency and collaboration in the education of each child.
